Purpose of the study:

The purpose of the study is to evaluate the utility of new user centered design software in collecting feedback on software user interface design to support user-centered design. This new software is being compared with the current standard for user-centered design, which are participatory design workshops.

You are being asked to participate in a research study because you are a NICU provider, and the example application is to help design an EHR-integrated neonatal service handoff list.

Procedures to be followed and approximate duration of the study:

You have been randomized to participate by using the new design software. Using the software, you will engage in designing and customizing a neonatal handoff document.

Prior to the session, you will be asked to complete a demographics survey and provide information about your role requirements. Following the session, we will ask you to complete a short post-activity survey to evaluate the usability of the process.

For this activity, imagine you are giving or receiving a printed neonatal service list handoff document prior to covering the intensive care unit overnight. You will be covering the service in your normal role with your normal support team present as well. You will be presented with a list of hypothetical neonatal patients who represent various neonatal conditions. Initially, only the patient name will be visible. As you select additional patient information, it will begin to populate the patient list accordingly. You will also be able to customize how that data is displayed. Once you have the handoff document customized to your preferences, click on the save and submit button to send your customizations to the design team.

The goal of this activity is for you to be able to share your specific preferences with a design team who is creating an EHR-integrated neonatal handoff tool. We will aggregate your individual submission with data from other neonatal providers to help determine how to create a neonatal handoff document that best supports you with the information and format you need to fulfill your specific role.

The estimated time required for participation using this new software is 10-30 minutes, although you may take as much time as you wish. You may also use the link in your email to return to the software to update or complete your selections at a future date.
